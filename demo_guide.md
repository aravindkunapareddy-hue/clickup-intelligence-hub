# ClickUp Content Intelligence — Client Demo Guide

This guide provides 4 distinct demo scenarios to showcase the versatility of the Content Intelligence Platform. Use these examples to demonstrate how the AI adapts its strategy based on the **Pod**, **Persona**, and **Funnel Stage**.

---

## Scenario 1: The "High-Traffic" SEO Play
**Goal**: Show top-of-funnel (TOFU) awareness and search authority.

*   **Pod**: SEO / Blog Pod
*   **Keyword**: `agile project management guide`
*   **Persona**: Junior Project Manager
*   **Funnel Stage**: TOFU — Awareness
*   **Business Goal**: Brand awareness & traffic
*   **Strategy**: Broad educational content, SEO-heavy metadata, and wide distribution on social to build a list.
*   **Key Differentiator**: High word count (2500+), focus on definitions and "what is" questions, "Coming soon" social plays.

---

## Scenario 2: The "Competitive Switch" YouTube Script
**Goal**: Show middle-of-funnel (MOFU) consideration and product comparison.

*   **Pod**: YouTube Pod
*   **Keyword**: `ClickUp vs Monday for small teams`
*   **Persona**: Startup Founder / CEO
*   **Funnel Stage**: MOFU — Consideration
*   **Business Goal**: Trial Signups
*   **Strategy**: Video-first approach. High-energy hook focusing on cost/efficiency. 
*   **Key Differentiator**: Visual script-ready angle, comparison-based SEO metadata, and YouTube-specific distribution tags.

---

## Scenario 3: The "Enterprise Trust" Case Study
**Goal**: Show bottom-of-funnel (BOFU) conversion and social proof.

*   **Pod**: Customer Marketing
*   **Keyword**: `scaling marketing operations with ClickUp`
*   **Persona**: VP of Marketing / Head of Ops
*   **Funnel Stage**: BOFU — Conversion
*   **Business Goal**: Enterprise Leads
*   **Strategy**: Narrative-driven, data-heavy, focusing on ROI and specific ClickUp features like Custom Fields or Dashboards.
*   **Key Differentiator**: Shorter, punchier word count but extremely high-intent keywords like "ROI", "Efficiency", "Scale".

---

## Scenario 4: The "High-Conversion" Landing Page
**Goal**: Show direct response and demand generation.

*   **Pod**: Demand Pod
*   **Keyword**: `best project management tool for creative agencies`
*   **Persona**: Agency Owner
*   **Funnel Stage**: MOFU/BOFU
*   **Business Goal**: Lead Generation
*   **Strategy**: Benefit-led copy with high-urgency CTAs. 
*   **Key Differentiator**: Focus on "Opening Hook" as a landing page headline, SEO metadata optimized for "Best [Category]" searches.

---

## How to Demo the "Saved Briefs" Workflow
1.  **Generate**: Run Scenario 1.
2.  **Save**: Click **💾 Save Brief**, name it "Agile Guide (SEO)".
3.  **New**: Click **➕ New Brief** to show a clean state for the client.
4.  **Generate**: Run Scenario 2.
5.  **Switch**: Click "Agile Guide (SEO)" in the Sidebar to show how the platform instantly restores the complete context for the previous project.
